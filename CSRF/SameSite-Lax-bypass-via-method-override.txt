Goal - perform a CSRF attack that changes the victim's email address. You should use the provided exploit server to host your attack.

Credentials - wiener:peter

- Log in with credentials wiener:peter and change email address

and in the form to change email do not include CSRF token
- Try to override the method by make a request:
/my-account/change-email?email=evil@gamil.com&_method=POST
==> SUCCESSFULLY
